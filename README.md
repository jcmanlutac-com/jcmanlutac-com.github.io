## About Me
I am an adaptable professional who believes that technology should serve as a helpful tool, rather than a challenging obstacle. With over 13 years of experience in technical support, I’ve learned that the best solutions aren't just powerful—they’re easy to use. I take the complex tasks of data and automation and transform them into simple, reliable systems that operate quietly in the background.

While I’m comfortable working with tools like Python, SQL, and PostgreSQL to manage databases or build web infrastructure (like my current project, Alaeh.PH), my real goal is to make sure your day-to-day operations feel effortless. Whether you need a database organized, a workflow automated, or a technical headache resolved, I’m here to bridge the gap and keep things running smoothly.

How I Can Help:

- Making Tech Simple: I specialize in explaining things in plain English and building tools that don't require a computer science degree to operate.

- Data & Automation: I can help you organize information and automate repetitive tasks using Python and SQL so you can spend less time on manual entry and more time on your business.

- Reliable Support: Having spent over a decade helping users at Jotform, I prioritize patience, clear communication, and finding the right fix the first time.

- Web & Database Maintenance: From keeping your web infrastructure healthy to managing cloud-based data (like Neon/PostgreSQL), I handle the technical details so you don't have to.

I view myself as a partner in your success. I focus on being approachable and easy to talk to, ensuring that even if you aren't "tech-savvy," you feel confident and supported every step of the way. I'm just a message away whenever you need a steady hand to help things click into place.
